Empowr'ing People

At Empowr, we believe that technology should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to make everyday computing, gaming, work, and creativity more inclusive for disabled people by offering products that adapt to the user - not the other way around.

Who We Are

We are a specialist assistive technology store focused on providing high-quality, adaptive tech solutions for people with a wide range of disabilities and access needs. Whether someone has reduced mobility, visual impairment, chronic pain, or neurological conditions, we aim to offer tools that help them use technology comfortably, confidently, and independently.

Our team research and curate products that are designed with accessibility in mind - from larger keys and brighter colours, to fully hands-free control systems. We work to remove barriers, not add new ones.

Our Commitment to Our Customers

We know that no two people have the same needs. Our goal is to help customers find the right combination of tools so they can work, learn, game and communicate in the way that suits them best. As our product range grows, we continue to look for new solutions that remove barriers and promote digital inclusion.

If you have any questions or specific accessibility requirements, please visit our Contact Us page - we're always happy to help.
